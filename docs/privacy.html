Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

ORCHESTR A320 ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

The App:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other device sensors
  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Operates entirely offline on your device

Data Storage

All checklist progress and app state is stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted anywhere and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
