Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app a replacement for official Airbus documentation?

No. ORCHESTR A320 is a training and reference tool designed to supplement your studies. Always refer to your operator's approved FCOM, QRH, and SOPs for actual flight operations.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. The app works entirely offline. All checklists, procedures, and the 3D cockpit are stored locally on your device.

How do I reset my checklist progress?

Open any checklist, tap the menu button (three dots) in the top right corner, and select "Reset All" to uncheck all items.

Which A320 variant does this app cover?

The app covers the A320-200 (CEO) and A320neo family. Performance data, limitations, and systems descriptions cover both variants where applicable.

Is the 3D cockpit accurate?

The 3D cockpit is a representative model for training and familiarization purposes. Instrument positions and labels are based on the standard A320 cockpit layout. Individual airline configurations may vary.

Does the app collect my data?

No. The app collects zero data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
